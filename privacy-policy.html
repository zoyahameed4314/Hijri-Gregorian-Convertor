Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At the Bidirectional Hijri-Gregorian Date Converter Tool, we take your privacy exceptionally seriously. This page outlines our data collection and protection policies.

1. Zero Server-Side Data Collection

This application is designed as a fully static, client-side utility. The mathematical conversions performed by this tool are executed entirely within your device's browser using native vanilla JavaScript. Absolutely no user data, date choices, search parameters, or personal identifier metrics are stored, logged, or transmitted over server pipelines.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use any tracking cookies, third-party analytics pixels, or session recording scripts on this domain. Your visit to this utility is entirely anonymous. Because the site operates independently of a backend database, there is no mechanism for us to capture or monetize your usage data.

3. External Links

This tool serves as a utility provided by a parent travel brand and may contain outbound anchor links pointing to our partner networks or primary travel booking domains. Once you click on a third-party link and leave this static directory, our Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal data, our service naturally complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and international equivalents. The tool is entirely safe for users of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this static Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect operational changes or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be permanently hardcoded into this HTML document, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be amended accordingly.
